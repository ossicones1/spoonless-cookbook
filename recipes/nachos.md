# Nachos

## Recipe

1. Lay corn chips on an oven tray lined with baking paper
2. Sprinkle with cheese and put into a 150 C oven
3. Rinse a tin of lentils or black beans and put into a saucepan with a tin of diced or crushed tomatoes with herbs
4. Add spice to bean mix if desired (e.g. chilli or paprika) and heat through gently stirring occasionally
5. Remove nachos by picking up baking paper and transferring to a plate
6. Put bean mix in a bowl
7. Break off nacho pieces and dip in bean mix

## Tips

For easier version use salsa in place of making bean mix
